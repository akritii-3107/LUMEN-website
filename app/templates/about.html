At LUMEN, we are dedicated to bring out the beauty of Physics and the applicability of Electronics through a variety of events and activities. Moreover, we aim at bringing the students of Physics and Electronics together and forming a close knit community which provides a platform where each individual gets to learn, grow and share knowledge and ideas. We wish to ensure that learning is inclusive, that is, we cater to the diverse interests of the students and provide them numerous learning opportunities both in the domain of academics as well as skill development. In addition to this, we wish to assist the students to equip themselves with good communication, interpersonal and networking skills to make them the quintessential all- rounders.
